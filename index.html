Guitar

The guitar course offers comprehensive lessons for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced players. Each class has a duration of 45 minutes, and students can attend either in-person or virtually.

The course structure includes various guitar techniques, music theory, and practical playing exercises to help students master the instrument. Recorded video lessons are available for students who wish to review content or catch up on missed classes. The course starts every month, with flexible timings to accommodate students' schedules, and the fee for the course is $200 per month, which includes access to recorded lessons and live sessions.
